FAQ

How does this calculator estimate quantity?

Enter your dimensions and rates, and the calculator applies standard construction formulas instantly.

How is price estimated?

The tool multiplies material quantity by a country-adjusted USD unit price for a fast budget view.

Can I switch between Metric and Imperial?

Yes. Unit selector toggles metric/imperial input interpretation and output display without reloading.

Are these prices exact?

No. Prices are approximate planning values and vary by supplier, region, and project conditions.
